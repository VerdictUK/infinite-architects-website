Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Summary: We respect your privacy. We collect minimal data, never sell your information, and use cookies only for essential functionality and anonymous analytics.

1. Who We Are

This website promotes Infinite Architects: Intelligence, Recursion, and the Creation of Everything by Michael Darius Eastwood. The site is operated from London, United Kingdom.

For privacy enquiries, contact: privacy@infinitearchitects.io

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Newsletter signup: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.
  • Contact forms: If you contact us, we collect the information you provide.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics data: We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This includes pages visited, time on site, and general geographic region (country-level only).
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, and screen resolution to ensure the site displays correctly.

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment information (purchases are handled by Amazon)
  • We do not track individual users across websites
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you our newsletter (if you subscribed)
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To improve the website experience
  • To understand aggregate visitor patterns

4. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies: Remember your cookie consent preference
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Preference cookies: Remember if you have seen certain elements (like popups)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The site will still function, though some features may be limited.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel: Website hosting
  • Google Fonts: Typography
  • Amazon: Book sales (clicking purchase links takes you to Amazon's site)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Unsubscribe from communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@infinitearchitects.io.

7. Data Retention

  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: Aggregated data retained for up to 26 months
  • Contact enquiries: Up to 2 years after last communication

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal data

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
